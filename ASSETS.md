# Asset replacement guide

The shipped `.webp` files are original, locally generated concept compositions. They are intentionally lightweight and ensure the project never depends on remote media.

For a production-quality portfolio case study, replace them with licensed photography while keeping the filenames and approximate 8:5 crop:

## Rooms (`public/images/rooms`)

- `mountain.webp` — south-facing guest room, early morning, mountain horizon.
- `city.webp` — city-facing room at blue hour.
- `club.webp` — intimate Club room in deep timber and stone.
- `executive.webp` — suite with separate living area.
- `grand.webp` — wide residential suite, evening atmosphere.

## Dining (`public/images/dining`)

- `vista.webp` — bright restaurant with open kitchen.
- `skybar.webp` — elevated lounge at dusk.
- `seven.webp` — dark bar with precise table lighting.
- `private.webp` — private dining room for 8–12 guests.

## Wellness (`public/images/wellness`)

- `pool.webp` — indoor pool with dark stone, reflections and restrained haze.

## Optional additions

The `public/images/hotel` and `public/images/almaty` folders are prepared for licensed lobby and destination photography. The 3D tower is procedural; no third-party model is required. `public/models` and `public/textures` are reserved for a future licensed GLB or compressed KTX2 assets.
